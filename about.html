हर किसान की पसंद — कृष्णा इंडस्ट्रीज

Founded in 1988 from a small workshop in Ballabgarh, Faridabad, Krishna Industries has grown into a trusted name in fluid management engineering. What began as a vision to solve India's agricultural water challenges is now a multi-state operation serving thousands of farmers and businesses.

Today we manufacture premium Submersible Pump Sets, Self-Priming Monoblock Pumps, Shallow Well Pumps, and our proprietary VACTOS Control Panels — all built with 101% pure copper winding, SKF bearings, and tested under ISO 9001:2015 quality standards.
